DenCity TraQR

For a safe and better new normal travel.

Purpose

Based on the suggestions and concerns of citizens regarding the use of QR code particularly in small establishments. It is required even small stores/establishments to have QR scanner for customers not only to track the customers but also to limit the availability and implement safety protocol inside the establishment. It is also to avoid the problem of false identity, that people may use false identification to enter facilities that do not use QR identification just to avoid contact-trace.

Web App Slides

Since our web application alerts people of the population density of certain establishments and places, DencityTraQR helps people plan out their trips and travels. It also eliminates the time to be wasted and uses the time instead to give people alternative options and plans. As for the second functionality, DencityTraQR can help people save time and effort finding existing QRs.

Web App Diagram

DencityTraQR is a web application that monitors the population density of each place and establishment in Davao city with the use of the data from contact tracing providers and with digital maps. This is to ensure that customers can have the necessary information before going outside and for business owners to create plans to effectively control the visitors in their shops. Aside from that, it provides a convenient way to store multiple QR codes for various areas through the My QR feature

Web App

Our solution is to build a web application that will track the number of visitors in a specific place using the entry notifications sent by the QR code providers / local contact tracing systems. At the same time, provide a place for users to store their contact tracing QR codes in the website.
